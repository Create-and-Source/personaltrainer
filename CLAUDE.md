# STOA — Wellness Creator Platform

## What This Is
Stoa is a wellness creator platform. Personal trainers, hypnotherapists, pilates instructors, meditation guides, yoga teachers — they all get their own space, and their clients use ONE app for everything. Your practitioners collaborate on you. Your whole wellness team sees you as a whole person.

## Brand & Design Direction

### Aesthetic: SKIMS White meets FORM App meets Editorial Wellness
- Photography-driven UI — every screen should feel like a lifestyle brand
- Warm skin tones, cream backgrounds, natural lighting
- Full-bleed image cards with serif text overlaid
- NOT a tech app. NOT a SaaS dashboard. This is a wellness experience.

### Colors
- Background: warm white `#FAF9F7`
- Surface: pure white `#FFFFFF`
- Text primary: `#1A1816` (rich black, not harsh)
- Text secondary: `#6B6560`
- Text muted: `#A8A29E`
- Accent: warm gold `#C9A96E`
- Borders: barely visible `#EDEBE8`
- Night mode: true black `#0D0D0D` with gold accents

### Typography
- Headlines: Playfair Display (serif, editorial, elegant)
- Body: Figtree (clean sans-serif)
- Monospace labels: Source Code Pro

### Visual Rules
- NO EMOJIS anywhere. Ever. Use minimal SVG icons or nothing.
- Lots of whitespace — let everything breathe
- Shadows and soft glow effects on cards
- Cards have 20px border radius
- Buttons are pill-shaped with gold glow
- When color shows up, it means something
- Photography should feel warm, natural, inclusive, aspirational
- Serif headlines overlaid on images — like a magazine

### Tone & Copy
- Empowering, not aggressive. "Movement" not "Workout." "Nourishment" not "Diet." "Stillness" not "Meditation."
- Affirmations woven throughout: "I am becoming the person I was always meant to be."
- Quotes like: "Her peace speaks louder than the noise ever could." "Unbothered. Unmatched." "She's not here to impress."
- Jim Rohn goal philosophy. Neville Goddard scripting. Wayne Dyer "I AM." Rhonda Byrne gratitude. Dr. Joe Dispenza visualization.

## Photography Needed

### Hero Categories (full-bleed cards on home screen)
1. **Movement** — Woman in neutral/cream activewear doing pilates, yoga, or stretching. Warm natural light. Clean background. Skin tones visible. Editorial feel.
2. **Nourishment** — Fresh greens, whole foods, clean eating. Hands preparing food. Colander with spinach. Warm tones, natural materials.
3. **Stillness / Connection** — Woman meditating, journaling, or in peaceful moment. Hands holding sage, crystals, tea. Warm earth tones. Intentional, not posed.
4. **Mind** — Close-up of someone breathing with eyes closed. Soft focus. Warm light on skin.

## App Structure

### Client Tabs (mobile-first)
- **Home** — Editorial hero cards (Movement, Nourishment, Stillness), daily affirmation, habits, on-demand preview
- **Train** — Workouts, exercise library, on-demand classes
- **Mind** — Breathing, journal wall, soundscapes, audio library, micro-lessons
- **Progress** — Weight, body comp, habits, manifestation log, goal wheel
- **Profile** — Settings, integrations, theme toggle

### Key Features
- Vision boards with pinned images and affirmations
- Goal wheel (Jim Rohn life categories)
- Neural pathway habit visualization (grooves that deepen with streaks)
- Before/after photos (private, shared with practitioner only)
- On-demand class library (like Netflix for wellness)
- Live streaming sessions
- AI coaching with practitioner voice cloning (ElevenLabs)
- Merch store per practitioner (dropship)
- Shared practitioner notes
- Spotify/YouTube integration
- Courses marketplace

## Tech Stack
- React 19 + Vite
- React Router
- Capacitor (iOS native)
- Vercel deployment
- No CSS frameworks — all inline styles with theme token system
